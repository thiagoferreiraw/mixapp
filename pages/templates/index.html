â€‹Strategic Partnerships

The more Brigid MIXs is able to inspire collaboration from education institutions, cultural and social centers, and other organizations, the faster Brigid MIXs will provide communities around the world access to: healthy, fun and insightful opportunities for cultural exchanges!.

Our partners collaborate by: 
  • - Filling gaps in Brigid MIXs Management Team
  • - Providing students to host MIXs in exchange for credit and recognition
  • - Offering space at their location for Brigid MIXs' activities.
  • - Providing free or discounted products and services. 
  • - Contributing economic resources to invest in the people and technology in order to grow Brigid MIXs exponentially. 
Any other ideas are strongly encouraged and welcomed!
