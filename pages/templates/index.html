How it works

MIXs are activities with people who are traveling in your city, or with people in the city you're traveling to.

All MIXs have a maximum of 8 participants (half local people, half travelers) All MIXs are located in public space like parks, social center, bars, etc.

All MIXs have moderators (MIX Mods) who help with language barriers, encourage everyone's involvement and lead a healthy exchange.

Practice English with an American, share a meal with a Brazilian, learn yoga pose with a japanese or make beer with a German. MIX themes are up to your interests and imagination!

Get involved!
