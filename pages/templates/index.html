​Mutual Interest eXchanges

MIXs are food, music, photography and other themed activities, connecting local people with travelers in public space!

We're creating an online community where anyone can host or participate in an activity of their choice, as long as you follow the Brigid MIXs Model​

The Brigid MIXs' Community

Find or host MIX activities in your city or a city you're traveling to!

Brigid MIXs is testing on three continents, with 22 volunteers in 8 countries. We're soft launching in up to 50 cities by the end of November, and the hard-launch is set for another 500 cities by end of April, 2018. Brigid MIXs' next milestone is 20k active monthly participants, and 100 million in 10 years!

Be an advocate for cultural exchange in your community by participating as a Brigid MIXs' Ambassador or MIX Guide.

​Cultural exchange is the seed to cultural progression; help us progress the world's culture!

​Strategic Partnerships

The more Brigid MIXs is able to inspire collaboration from education institutions, cultural and social centers, and other organizations, the faster Brigid MIXs will provide communities around the world access to: healthy, fun and insightful opportunities for cultural exchanges!.

Our partners collaborate by: 
  • - Filling gaps in Brigid MIXs Management Team
  • - Providing students to host MIXs in exchange for credit and recognition
  • - Offering space at their location for Brigid MIXs' activities.
  • - Providing free or discounted products and services. 
  • - Contributing economic resources to invest in the people and technology in order to grow Brigid MIXs exponentially. 
Any other ideas are strongly encouraged and welcomed!
