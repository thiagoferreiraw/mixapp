What are MIXs?

MIX activities are small groups of local people and travelers who unite because of a shared interest.

Practice English with an American, share a meal with a Brazilian, learn a new yoga pose with a Japanese or make beer with a German. ​MIX themes are up to your interests and imagination!

The MIX elements

The perfect connection for fun, safe and insightful MIXs!

- MIXs are never more than 8 participants, half locals and half travelers.

- MIXs are only located in public spaces like parks, cultural/social centers, concerts, sports stadiums, hostels, colleges/universities, etc.

- MIX Moderators are always present to help with language barriers and encourage a healthy exchange.

Get involved!

Participant

Members who reserve to participate in a MIX activity in their city, or a city they're traveling to. Participants share what they know about a particular art, skill or hobby, related to the MIX theme.

FIND A MIX
Moderator

Members who choose a MIX theme and location. Mods are the liaison between the local people and travelers, helping with language barriers and encouraging everyone's involvement.

#MODLIFE
Ambassador

Members who take an active role in their community to promote MIXs. Ambassadors establish and maintain strategic partnerships, as well as host special MIXs with Moderators.
